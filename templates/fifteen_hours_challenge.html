INTRODUCTION
Participants will take part in an immersive, time-based role-play activity over a course of 15 hours. In their respective groups, the participants will engage in activities that put their knowledge and skills acquired over the previous days to the test. It is a race against time where participants must work effectively as a team, think critically and creatively, deploy limited resources and surpass physical and mental limits.
