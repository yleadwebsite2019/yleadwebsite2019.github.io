As generations pass, the youth of today will soon be the ones leading the society, being at the forefront of its progress and development. The future of our society holds uncertainties and issues of great complexity that are not the easiest to overcome. How do we prepare youths for the future?

Through Y.LEAD, we aspire to develop future leaders. We hope to empower youths by equipping them with sufficient 21st century competencies to tackle future problems and resolve them through innovative means.

CHANGEMAKERS
A Change maker is one who desires positive change in the world and, by gathering knowledge and resources, makes that change happen.

1. Tenacious about the greater good
2. Team player; belief that all can be change makers
3. Dedicated to solving the problem at hand

Anyone can be a changemaker, they can come from every walk of life, regardless of status, power, influence, gender or race. When every small change is a change for the better, the slightest of all change can make you a changemaker.

We are living in a world that needs us more than ever before. With the ever changing political arena, the ever rising climate temperatures and the heightened terrorism threat levels, the world is in a perpetual state of chaos. SMART CITY
1. Efficiency
2. Sustainability
3. Interconnectivity

There is an increase in the number of cities migrating from rural to urban cities, this occurrence is known as rapid urbanisation.

A smart city is a developed urban area that creates sustainable economic development and high quality of life, where information technology plays a pivotal role in providing essential services to its residents multiple key areas - Healthcare, transport, environment, education, defense and economy.
