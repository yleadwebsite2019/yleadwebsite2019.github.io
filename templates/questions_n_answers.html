QUESTIONS & ANSWERS
Will couple dance be brought back for this year's mass dance?
We will not be bringing back the couple dance this year.
What does Y.LEAD aim to train Sec 3s in?
The objective of the event can be found on the website so do check it out!
What is the cultural appreciation segment about?
It will be an activity where we will get to learn the different cultures of the participants. There will be different booths set up and performancres put up by the participants from the various countries.
Can heels be worn to commencement night?
It will not be recommented to wear high heels as there will be quite a lot of movement throughtout the celebration night. In order to minimise accidents and injuries, wearing flats or normal sport shoes would be more suitable.
