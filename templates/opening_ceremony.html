INTRODUCTION
The opening ceremony marks the beginning of the Y.LEAD Seminar 2019. Participants will not only get to enjoy performances put up by students from River Valley High School, they will also be given an official introduction and preview of the seminar through the keynote address prepared by the Chairperson of the seminar. The ceremony will end with insightful sharings by the Guest-of-Honour, allowing participants to broaden their perspectives.
GUEST OF HONOUR

Mr Baey Yam Keng
Senior Parliamentary Secretary
Ministry of Transport & Ministry of Culture
Community and Youth
Mr Baey entered the Singapore Parliament in 2006 and was appointed as Parliamentary Secretary for Ministry of Culture, Community and Youth in October 2015. In May 2018, he was appointed as Senior Parliamentary Secretary for Ministry of Culture, Community and Youth & Ministry of Transport.i He also sits on the National Youth Council and is Director of Chinese Development Assistance Council.

Mr Baey started his career in 1995 in the public sector, where he served in the Economic Development Board, Ministry of Trade & Industry, Ministry of Information, Communications & the Arts and National Arts Council. He switched to the corporate sector in 2006 as Vice-President, Corporate Marketing & Corporate Social Responsibility, at real estate company CapitaLand Limited, and was concurrently General Manager of the CapitaLand Hope Foundation. He joined international communications consultancy Hill+Knowlton Strategies in 2009 and was its Singapore Managing Director from 2011 to 2012. He was also an adjunct lecturer at Nanyang Technological University from 2013 to 2015.

Mr Baey has over 20 years of theatrical production experience as the founding President of The ETCeteras. He has produced more than 10 original plays.
