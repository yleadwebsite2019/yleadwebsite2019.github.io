15 HOURS CHALLENGE
15HC 2019
Arrival of the Extraterrestrials
Lohel little earthling, we are from A.I, a planet in a galaxy far far away. On Day 4 of your camp, we will arrive and place you in an immersive time-based challenge.

In your respective groups, you will be faced with various challenges that will put your knowledge and the skills you have acquired in the past few days to the test. It is a race against time where you must think critically and creatively, synergise as a team, work around the limitations of resources and surpass your personal limits.

We need your help to save our planet.
15 hours. No mistakes. No hesitation.
