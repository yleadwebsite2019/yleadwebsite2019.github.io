INTRODUCTION
As part of the Seminar, participants will undergo a specially tailored experiential Leadership-Character Development Programme developed by River Valley High School, Singapore. The customised activities will not only enhance participantsâ€™ leadership qualities, but also provide them with opportunities to build friendships with other student leaders, learn vigilance and cultivate adaptability, effective communication and self-motivation. There will also be a strong emphasis on the inculcation of values, in line with our aim to nurture leaders who make morally sound decisions that are beneficial to others to achieve a greater good.
PERSONALITY PROFILING AND TEAM DYNAMICS
Every team comprises of different kinds of leaders - Interactor, Initiator, Ideator and Implementer. Participants can learn more about their own leadership style and also discover more about their team dynamics such as the strengths and weaknesses of their team. Most importantly, on the first day of the Y.LEAD seminar, participants can start to discover and recognise the types of leadership styles present among their members.Our workshop is critical in laying a foundation for the participants and teams to build up their team spirit and identity in Y.LEAD.
LEADERSHIP 101
Through a variety of unconventional and unique station games, participants will have a chance to put whatever they have learnt in our Personality Profiling and Team Dynamics Workshop into action. The teams will have to work cohesively and closely with one another and communicate effectively in order to complete and succeed in several station games. The engaging and purposeful games would allow the team to not only bond and have fun, but also further develop their leadership values as well as hone their leadership skills.
